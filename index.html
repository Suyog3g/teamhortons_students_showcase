About Event

The SDPA Industry Night for 2023 will showcase student projects and activities from the Interactive Media Design Program.

Meet and mingle with faculty and students, tour the facilities, and hear about upcoming projects and programs in the School of Digital and Performing Arts.

Interactive Media Design

Explore the dynamic world of Interactive Media Design! This course delves into the art and science of creating engaging digital experiences, including web design, user interface (UI) and user experience (UX) design, multimedia content, and interactive applications.
